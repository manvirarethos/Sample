    It’s easy to register with MyHealthRecord

    To sign up for MyHealthRecord, you need to fill a registration form with following details:

    1. Registration: Fill the registration form for all mandatory fields. Mobile number, is used to send the OTP.

    2. Confirm Mobile OTP : Fill the OTP received on your mobile number for final submission.

    3. Complete: Once you submit the OTP you will complete the registration process.
